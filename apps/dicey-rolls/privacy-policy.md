# Privacy Policy — Dicey Rolls

*Last updated: June 2026*

## Summary

Dicey Rolls does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data stays on your device.

## Data Storage

- **Roll history** and **saved dice sets** are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
- **Settings** (theme, sounds, haptics preferences) are stored locally using UserDefaults.
- No data is sent to any external server.

## Analytics & Tracking

- The app does **not** use any analytics SDKs.
- The app does **not** track users across apps or websites.
- `NSPrivacyTracking` is set to `false` in the app's privacy manifest.

## Purchases

- In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework.
- Dicey Rolls does not handle or store payment information.

## Advertising

- The free tier reserves a banner slot for future advertising. No ad SDK is currently integrated in this version.
